About Me

My name is Aaron Key and I'm a full stack developer from North Carolina. I started with some simple static HTML sites, bought several books, and eventually started working with React, NodeJS, and RESTful APIs. I made some simple but neat projects just for fun, and realized I enjoy making things work and look nice and decided to pursue a career in web development. I've made some smaller simple websites for local businesses and several apps for personal use in things that interest me. In my spare time when I'm not fixing bugs or working on a new project, I like to play guitar, video games, and go on adventures with my wife and our two dogs Goku and Teddy. Goku is a Shiba Inu and Teddy is a Goldendoodle
